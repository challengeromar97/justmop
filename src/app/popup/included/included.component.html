Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?

    Our captain will bring free laundry bags for you to fill up. We have two bag sizes for the following: Wash and Fold (Small/Big), Ironing only (Small/Big), Wash and Iron bag (Small/Big). We will ensure that all your items will be cleaned according to what you have requested.

  • Do you offer dry cleaning?

    Yes, just tell us which items you need dry cleaned, and we’ll bring them back smelling great and freshly ironed. Dry cleaning is priced per piece and you can see the detailed price list above.

  • Do you have a minimum charge?

    No, we do not have any minimum charge.

  • Do you have delivery fees?

    Pick-up and delivery is free of charge.
